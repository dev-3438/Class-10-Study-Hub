Key Terms & Definitions

Life Processes (Class 10 NCERT)

Autotrophic Nutrition

Nutrition in which organisms make their own food (plants) via photosynthesis.

Heterotrophic Nutrition

Nutrition in which organisms depend on other organisms for food (animals, fungi, some bacteria).

Respiration

Process of breaking down food to release energy for life processes.

Aerobic Respiration

Respiration using oxygen; glucose → CO₂ + H₂O + energy.

Anaerobic Respiration

Respiration without oxygen; produces less energy and by-products like lactic acid or ethanol.

Xylem

Vascular tissue in plants that transports water and minerals from roots to leaves.

Phloem

Vascular tissue that transports food from leaves to other parts of the plant.

Blood

Fluid that transports oxygen, nutrients, and wastes in humans.

Excretion

Process of removal of metabolic wastes from the body to maintain homeostasis.

Homeostasis

Maintaining stable internal conditions necessary for survival (temperature, pH, water balance).
