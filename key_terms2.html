Key Terms & Definitions

Light - Reflection and Refraction (Class 10 NCERT)

Reproduction

The biological process by which organisms produce new individuals of their species.

Sexual Reproduction

Involves two parents; offspring inherit traits from both parents via gametes (sperm and egg).

Asexual Reproduction

Involves a single parent; offspring are genetically identical to the parent.

Binary Fission

A type of asexual reproduction where a single organism divides into two identical organisms.

Budding

A new organism develops from a bud or outgrowth on the parent organism.

Spore Formation

Some organisms produce spores that grow into new individuals under favorable conditions.

Fertilization

The fusion of male and female gametes to form a zygote, starting sexual reproduction.
