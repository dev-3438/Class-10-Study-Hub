Life Processes - Class 10 Science

Life Processes

Nutrition

Autotrophic: Photosynthesis (CO₂ + H₂O → Glucose + O₂)
Heterotrophic: Holozoic, Saprophytic, Parasitic

Respiration

Aerobic: Glucose + O₂ → CO₂ + H₂O + ATP
Anaerobic: Glucose → Lactic acid / Ethanol + Less energy

Transport

Humans: Blood, Heart, Vessels
Plants: Xylem (Water), Phloem (Food)

Excretion

Humans: Kidney, Lungs, Skin
Plants: Stomata, Lenticels, Leaves

Reproduction

Asexual: Binary fission, Budding, Fragmentation, Vegetative propagation
Sexual: Gametes fuse, Plants: Flower → Seed/Fruit, Humans: Sperm + Egg → Zygote

Survival

Homeostasis: Maintain internal balance
Variation: Sexual reproduction → helps adaptation

Memory Tip

Remember: "Nutrition, Respiration, Transport, Excretion, Reproduction" – main life processes!
